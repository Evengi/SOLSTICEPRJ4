All Aboard!

Escape from the mundane and embark on a journey of a lifetime with Solstice's "Big Bertha" cruise ship.

Experience the thrill of adventure as you explore the Caribbean islands, with each day offering new and exciting possibilities. From the moment you step onboard, you'll be transported to a world of wonder, with activities that will leave you breathless and begging for more.

Enjoy morning yoga as the sun rises over the crystal-clear waters, or paint a masterpiece while surrounded by stunning views. With aquatic cardio and live concerts, the ship provides non-stop entertainment, including a boulevard of shops and 24/7 access to a fully-equipped gym.

There's More?

As the sun sets, the ship comes alive with Latino dance classes, Tango lessons, and nightly musical performances, showcasing the incredible talents of the onboard entertainers.

The cruise also takes you to some of the most exotic and beautiful locations on the planet, with organized trips that will transport you back in time to learn about the traditional heritage of each location.

The Solstice cruise provides the perfect environment for creativity to flourish, rewarding guests with unforgettable memories and life-changing relationships.
